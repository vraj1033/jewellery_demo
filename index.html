Our Heritage

Crafting Dreams
Since 1987


Royal Gems Jewelry was born from a single vision — to bring unparalleled artistry and ethically sourced gems to the families of Ahmedabad. Founded by master goldsmith Rameshbhai Mehta on C.G. Road, our atelier has adorned brides, celebrated milestones, and become synonymous with trust across generations.

Every piece is conceived by our in-house designers, handcrafted by artisans who have perfected their craft over decades, and set with certified conflict-free diamonds and hallmarked 22K / 18K gold. We weave ancient Gujarati jewellery traditions with contemporary elegance to create heirlooms that outlast time.

From a delicate solitaire chosen at dawn to a grand bridal parure unveiled at dusk — each Royal Gems creation carries the weight of a story, the warmth of a memory.
